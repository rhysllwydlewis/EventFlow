How It Works
1
A partner signs up at /partner (hidden, noindex — share directly).
2
They receive a unique referral link like /auth?ref=p_XXXX&role=supplier to share with wedding suppliers.
3
When a referred supplier creates their first package (within 30 days), the partner earns +10 credits.
4
When a referred supplier makes their first successful subscription payment (within 30 days, non-trial), the partner earns +100 credits.
5
Use this page to view stats, adjust credits, and enable or disable any partner account.

Programme Features
🔒
Hidden portal
Partner pages are noindex/nofollow and not linked from public navigation.
🔄
Idempotent awards
Each bonus is awarded at most once per supplier — no double-payments on retries.
🛡️
Trial exclusion
£0 / trial Stripe invoices never trigger the subscription bonus.
📋
Full ledger
Every credit transaction is stored in partner_credit_transactions with an audit trail.
⚡
Manual adjustments
Add or deduct credits any time with a required audit note stored against the transaction.
🚫
Instant disable
Disabling a partner blocks all API access and stops new credit awards immediately.
